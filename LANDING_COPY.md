## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. Completely free. No credit card, no login. We show the audit results before asking for your email — never the other way around.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: We verify all vendor pricing pages weekly. Every number in the audit traces to an official source URL in our pricing changelog. If you find an error, email us and we update within 24 hours.

**Q: Who is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise — sourced from companies that over-purchased enterprise subscriptions. For teams with significant spend, we surface Credex as an additional savings channel beyond what the audit itself finds.

**Q: Does the AI generate the recommendations?**
A: No — deliberately. The audit engine is 100% deterministic rules-based code. Financial recommendations need to be reproducible, testable, and credible to a finance team. AI is only used for the brief personalised summary paragraph. Knowing when not to use AI is part of what makes this trustworthy.

**Q: What if my spend is already optimised?**
A: We tell you honestly. "You're spending well" is a real and frequent outcome. We won't manufacture savings that don't exist — that would make the tool useless as a decision aid.
