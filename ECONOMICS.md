## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

**Credex business model:** Buy AI enterprise credits at a discount from companies that over-purchased, resell at a price that's still 20-40% below retail.

**Estimated transaction size:** $8,000 (one quarter of enterprise AI tool subscription for a 20-person team — e.g., $800/mo × 10 months, bulk purchased upfront)

**Gross margin per transaction:** ~25% (Credex's spread between purchase and resale)

**Gross profit per transaction:** $8,000 × 25% = **$2,000**

**Customer LTV estimate (conservative):** Assume a startup that buys once re-purchases every 2 quarters as subscriptions renew. 2 purchases/year × $2,000 margin = **$4,000 LTV**

## Conversion funnel

```
Visitor lands on SpendLens
    × 40%  → completes audit form
    × 30%  → submits email (value shown first)
    × 15%  → opens follow-up email (standard cold email open rate)
    × 8%   → clicks Credex CTA in email or on results page
              (this segment is filtered to high-savings audits)
    × 30%  → books Credex consultation
    × 30%  → converts to credit purchase

End-to-end: visitor → purchase ≈ 0.4%
```

**For high-savings audits (>$500/mo) specifically:**
- Email capture rate: higher (~45%) because the result is dramatic
- Credex CTA click rate: higher (~20%) because the value prop is specific
- Consultation booking rate: higher (~40%)
- Close rate: similar (~35%)
- High-savings funnel: visitor → purchase ≈ 1.3%

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

**Credex revenue from SpendLens conversions:**

Revenue = Credex gross margin, not GMV.

Need to generate $1M/year in gross margin:
- $1M ÷ $2,000/purchase = **500 purchases/year**
- 500 purchases = 500/0.30 = ~1,670 consultations booked
- 1,670 consultations = 1,670/0.08 = ~20,875 Credex CTA clicks
- 20,875 clicks = ~70,000 email captures (clicks from ~30% of captures)
- 70,000 captures = ~235,000 audit completions (30% capture rate)
- 235,000 completions = ~590,000 visitors (40% completion rate)

**Required: 590,000 visitors over 18 months = ~32,800/month by Month 18**

Month 1 realistic: 2,000 visitors.
Required growth rate: ~20%/month compounding.

**What makes 20%/month possible:**
- Show HN provides initial velocity (Month 1 spike)
- Shareable audit URLs create organic word-of-mouth (coefficient ~0.3 — every 3 audits generates 1 additional visitor via sharing)
- Credex vendor relationships provide a distribution channel no competitor can replicate
- Blog content compounds: one "cursor vs copilot cost" post that ranks in Google is worth hundreds of visitors/month indefinitely

## Key sensitivities

| Variable | Bear | Base | Bull |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email capture rate | 15% | 30% | 45% |
| Consultation booking | 3% | 8% | 15% |
| Close rate | 15% | 30% | 50% |
| Avg. transaction | $4,000 | $8,000 | $15,000 |

**Bear case** for $1M ARR: requires 1.2M visitors by Month 18 — unlikely without paid distribution.
**Bull case:** $1M ARR achievable by Month 12 with strong vendor partnership distribution.
